Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TrackerVLR, a Discord bot focused on Valorant statistics and match tracking, collects, uses, stores, and protects limited service-related information required to operate.

Last updated: 2026

By using TrackerVLR, you acknowledge that certain technical and gameplay-related information may be processed solely for the purpose of delivering bot features, responding to commands, and maintaining service quality.

1. Information Collected

TrackerVLR may collect and process limited information necessary to provide its functionality. This may include Discord user IDs, server IDs, records of commands used, and Valorant player-related data submitted or requested through the bot in order to retrieve statistics, match summaries, and performance information.

The bot is not intended to collect unnecessary personal information beyond what is reasonably required for operation.

2. How Information Is Used

Information processed by TrackerVLR is used only to operate the bot and deliver its intended features. This includes identifying command context, responding to user requests, retrieving Valorant-related statistics, improving stability, preventing abuse, and maintaining technical logs required for support or diagnostics.

3. Data Sharing

TrackerVLR does not sell personal data and does not share collected information for marketing purposes. Information may only be disclosed when necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, enforce platform rules, or protect the integrity, security, and lawful operation of the bot.

4. Data Storage

Data may be stored temporarily or for a limited operational period to support command handling, caching, abuse prevention, troubleshooting, and service continuity. Retention duration may vary depending on the type of data and the legitimate technical need associated with it.

Stored information is kept only for as long as reasonably necessary for the functioning of TrackerVLR.

5. Data Removal

Where technically feasible and appropriate, data associated with the use of TrackerVLR may be deleted or removed after it is no longer required for operational, legal, or security purposes. Removing the bot from a server will generally prevent further server-based processing, though limited records may remain for a reasonable period in backups, logs, or abuse-prevention systems.

6. Third-Party Services

TrackerVLR relies on third-party services, including Discord infrastructure and external APIs that may provide Valorant-related data. Those services may process requests independently under their own terms and privacy policies. We are not responsible for the availability, content, or privacy practices of those third-party providers.

7. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to help protect information processed by TrackerVLR against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or loss. However, no internet-based service, API integration, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Changes to Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect legal, operational, technical, or feature-related changes. Continued use of TrackerVLR after updates become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

Questions or requests related to this Privacy Policy should be directed to the maintainers of TrackerVLR through the official support contact or designated server administration channel associated with the bot.

TrackerVLR uses data only for bot functionality, does not sell user data, and may interact with external Valorant-related APIs.
